Adopting AI/LLM with engineering discipline

Use AI to augment engineering workflows without introducing unchecked risk.

AI is most valuable when it becomes part of the engineering process, not a separate initiative. That means choosing use cases with clear productivity leverage and integrating them into existing workflows.

Targeted AI adoption

  • Automate routine work and common developer queries.
  • Use AI to support documentation, code review insights, and knowledge transfer.
  • Ensure human oversight remains central to decision-making.

Keeping adoption practical

Measure the impact of AI features and iterate based on real engineering feedback. Avoid chasing novelty at the expense of predictable value.

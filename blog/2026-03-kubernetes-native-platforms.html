    • Designing Kubernetes-native platform experiences

    Focus on patterns that are native to container platforms and easy for developers to consume.

    Kubernetes-native platforms should minimize custom work and maximize reusable conventions. That means building around standard APIs, deployment units, and operational patterns.

    Key design principles

    • Keep the platform aligned with Kubernetes primitives.
    • Provide clear onboarding and templates for common workloads.
    • Delegate cluster ownership while preserving consistency.

    Operational consistency

    Focus on consistent observability, deployment lifecycle, and configuration management so teams can move between projects without losing context.
